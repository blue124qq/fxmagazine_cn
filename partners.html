Slack Partners

Building a better way to work, together

Slack partners with the most innovative leaders in the industry to help teams achieve a better way to work.

The Slack partner ecosystem

Our partners offer a broad range of tools, services and solutions to help companies deploy Slack across the organisation and ensure teams get the most out of Slack.

Delight your teams

Remove the friction of work by seamlessly bringing the tools your teams use every day into Slack.

Unlock new opportunities

Leverage our partners’ capabilities and expertise to adopt better ways to collaborate within and outside of your organisation.

Extend your investment

Easily connect Slack to your existing IT architecture with our open platform.

Meet our technology partners

Slack integrates with the tools teams use daily – from finance and marketing automation to productivity and video conferencing – seamlessly streamlining your work in one place.

GSuite

A set of time-saving apps that helps you stay on top of your calendar, collaborate on files, update your status and more.

Salesforce

The world’s no. 1 CRM platform for businesses of all sizes that gives all departments a single shared view of every customer.

Workday

Leading ERP system for financial management, human resources and planning.

Zoom

A modern video communication platform that unifies meetings, webinars, conference rooms and phone communication.

Box

A secure platform for content management, workflow and collaboration.

Atlassian

A set of productivity tools that helps teams work smarter and faster together, including Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket and Trello.

Dropbox

The smart workspace that brings all of a team’s content together with the tools they love.

Zendesk

A customer service and engagement suite that helps customers better understand and serve their customers.

Meet our security and compliance partners

Slack integrates with the leading security and compliance providers so organisations can meet crucial requirements in the areas of data loss prevention, identity management, e-discovery and more.

View our App Directory

Okta

Cloud-based identity management platform, offering single sign-on, multi-factor authentication, lifecycle management (provisioning) and more.

McAfee

Offering real-time protection for enterprise data and users across all cloud services.

Symantec

Integrated cloud security solution offering data loss prevention (DLP) and cloud access security broker (CASB) services.

Onna

A central information access point that connects any data source to deliver a streamlined, simplified search and management experience.

Netskope

Industry-leading data loss prevention (DLP) with language-independent content inspection and support.

Smarsh

Leading data archiving provider that delivers specialised capture, archiving and discovery solutions across a range of communication channels.

Relativity

E-discovery software that offers the tools needed to manage litigation, investigations and government requests.
