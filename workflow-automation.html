What is Workflow Builder?

A faster, smarter way of working

Create workflows that automate routine actions and communication, all in a matter of minutes.

Unblock productivity

Spend less time tracking down the people needed to get work done. Automatically collect and route info to the right teams, all in Slack where you’re already working.

Avoid unnecessary delays

Skip the follow-ups and back-and-forth. Easily standardise how you collect and share info in advance so you can find exactly what you need when you need it.

Get going quickly

Take control of your working day. In a just a few minutes, create a customised workflow that matches the way you work – or start with an example below.
